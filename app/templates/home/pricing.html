Frequent Asked questions

1. What do brvcase offer me ?

We offer cloud-based brief management software solutions that makes running your firm, organizing cases, and collaborating with clients from one place possible; giving you more time with clients and less paper work.

2. What Do I get from brvcase?

At the most basic level, brvcase helps put all your accounts and contact information in a central and accessible place, so you accelerate and streamline the sales process. For instance, instead of being mired down in yellow sticky note reminders or buried in spreadsheets and Google Docs, you can move leads through your sales team quickly and easily, as a team..

3. Is brvcase worth the value for small Agency?

Yes. Even if your customer base is small, no one can keep every interaction — ones that have happened, or need to happen — memorized and organized. brvcase is a powerful business tool that streamlines processes, gives valuable business insights, and virtually connects employees at your company so they can collaborate as a team, in real time. That’s something businesses of any size can use.

4. How do you know your business need a brvcase?

Below are some clues that can help you in your decision.
Signs you need BRVCASE

    If anything on this short checklist sounds familiar, brvcase might be worth exploring to help your business find, win, and keep customers more efficiently.

  • Your sales teams are often on the road

  • You have a patchwork of apps that you call CRM but they are not really connected on top of a single database or “system of record”
